I am always looking for ambitious and highly motivated students with strong math and implementation skills. If you are currently enrolled at Ohio State and are interested to do research with me on natural language processing, information extraction, machine learning, and/or social media analysis, feel free to send a short email with 1) your C.V. or resume and 2) a brief description of what areas you are interested in and what you are hoping to get out of our interaction. Generally I like to work with students who have some prior experience with machine learning and/or natural language processing; having some previous interaction in a class is a huge plus. Students outside OSU are welcome to send email as well, but admissions decisions are made by committee. I get many of these emails and unfortunately I can't reply to all of them - that doesn't mean I haven't your note!
